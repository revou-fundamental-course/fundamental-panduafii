We are March, We are Solid!

March Steel Industries is a leading iron and steel company, reinforcing the foundations of the construction and manufacturing industries for over two decades. Committed to quality and innovation, March Steel Industries has become a symbol of trust and reliability in the global market.

Our Profile:

Founded in 2000 and headquartered in Jakarta, Indonesia, March Steel prioritizes integrity, sustainability, and continuous improvement. Utilizing cutting-edge technology and efficient processes, we ensure our products meet and exceed stringent industry standards.

Vision and Mission:

Our vision is to be the most respected leader in the steel industry, driving innovation and efficiency through technology and human resources. Our mission is to provide superior products and services, support sustainable infrastructure growth, and strengthen our communities.
